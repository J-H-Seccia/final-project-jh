Dice Game

Directions:

Press the roll button to roll yours and the computers dice. After you'ved rolled 3 times, whoever has the highest score wins! Press the reset button to reset the game and your scores.

Modifiers:

1. If any player's dice roles a 1, that rounds score will be zero.

2. If any player rolls a pair, the sum of the 2 dice will be multiplied by 2.
